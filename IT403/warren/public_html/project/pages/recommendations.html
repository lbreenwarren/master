Music Has the Right to Children
Boards of Canada

A seminal record in the IDM landscape, Boards of Canada's debut album is often glanced over in discussion of classic electronic records, being overshadowed by the likes of Aphex Twin and Daft Punk, but beyond a doubt deserves a seat at the table when talking greats. Music Has the Right to Children finds the perfect balance of fast paced dance music and trance inducing ambient, the whole time deftly avoiding tropes of both, staying persistently interesting the whole time. This is not chill beats to study to, this is chill beats to listen to
