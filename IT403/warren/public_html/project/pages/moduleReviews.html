Make Noise Maths

The make noise maths is an essential module in my anybody's rack, it can function as an envelope generator, an LFO, a gate generator, a clock, an oscillator and thats not even the creative stuff. Any time I find myself wondering how I could achieve a certain sound or movement in a patch, Maths is the first module I look to.
